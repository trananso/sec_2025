Job Title: Retail Sales Associate
Company: UrbanStyle Clothing Co.
Location: Mississauga, ON (In-store)
Employment Type: Part-time
Compensation: $17.00 per hour

Overview:
UrbanStyle Clothing Co. is looking for a friendly, reliable Retail Sales Associate to join our team at Square One. You will help customers find products they love, keep the store organized, and support day-to-day operations.

Key Responsibilities:
- Greet customers and assist with product selection
- Operate the POS system to process sales, returns, and exchanges
- Restock shelves and maintain visual merchandising standards
- Keep fitting rooms and sales floor clean and organized
- Assist with inventory counts as required

Requirements (Must-Have):
- Strong customer service and communication skills
- Ability to stand for extended periods (up to 4–5 hours)
- Basic math skills and comfort using a point-of-sale system
- Availability for at least 3 shifts per week, including evenings and weekends

Nice-to-Have:
- Previous retail or customer-facing experience
- Interest in fashion and styling

Work Hours:
- Shifts typically range from 4–8 hours
- Schedule includes evenings, weekends, and holidays; specific schedule provided at least 2 weeks in advance

Benefits:
- 30% employee discount on all merchandise
- On-the-job training and opportunities for advancement
- Eligibility for performance-based bonuses after 6 months

AI Use in Hiring:
- UrbanStyle does not use AI to make hiring decisions.
- We may use automated tools for resume organization, but final decisions are made by store management.

Existing Vacancy:
- This posting is for an existing vacancy.

Application Process:
Please submit your resume and your general availability. Interviews will be conducted in person at the store.

Accommodation:
UrbanStyle Clothing Co. welcomes and encourages applications from people with disabilities. Accommodations are available on request for candidates taking part in all aspects of the selection process.
